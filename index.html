Mark Zuckerberg

Bio

Mark Zuckerberg is the co-founder of Facebook. He started the social media platform in 2004 while studying at Harvard University.

Highlights

  • Co-founded Facebook at age 19
  • CEO of Meta Platforms
  • One of the youngest billionaires
  • "Move fast and break things."

Why I Admire Him:

Visionary Innovator – He co-founded Facebook at just 19, turning a college project into one of the world’s biggest tech companies.

Persistence – Despite criticism and challenges, he kept pushing forward and evolving the platform into Meta, showing resilience.

Commitment to Learning – He’s known for taking on personal challenges like learning new languages and reading many books each year.

Philanthropy – Through the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, he’s committed billions to improving education, health, and equality around the world.
